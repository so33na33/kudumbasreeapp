Kudumbasree Loans


Kudumbashree is currently in the process of implementing hon Chief Minister’s ‘Sahayahastham’ (Helping Hands) loan scheme declared by the Government of Kerala. Kudumbashree NHGs will be given interest free loans as indicated in the guidelines. Kudumbashree received the Government order regarding the same on 05.04.2020 and a detailed guideline by the Executive Director has been prepared. The loan applications will be submitted to banks by April 15th.
